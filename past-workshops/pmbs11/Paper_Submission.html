2nd International Workshop on

Performance Modeling, Benchmarking and Simulation of High Performance Computer Systems (PMBS11)

held as part of SC11, Seattle, WA, USA, November 13th 2011, in association with ACM SIGMETRICS

 
 

Papers submitted for the PMBS11 Workshop should be original, unpublished work which is no longer than 8-pages in length and formatted to the ACM SIG Alternate style. All submitted papers will be peer reviewed and assessed on technical achievement, novelty and workshop relevance. Papers should be submitted via the PMBS11 EasyChair submission system using the link below.


All papers will be published in a special edition of the ACM Performance Evaluation Review (PER). Authors grant ACM permission to publish their paper in print and digital formats.


Selected authors will also be invited to submit extended work for inclusion in a special edition of The Computer Journal and IEEE Computer Architecture Letters.
