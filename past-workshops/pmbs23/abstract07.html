Hardware Specialization: Estimating Monte Carlo Cross-Section Lookup Kernel Performance and Area

Kazutomo Yoshii, John R. Tramm, Bryce Allen, Andrew Siegel, Pete Beckman
Argonne National Laboratory Lemont, IL, USA

Tomohiro Ueno, Kentaro Sano
RIKEN Center for Computational Science, Kobe, Hyougo, Japan

Hardware specialization is one of the promising directions in the post-Moore era. It is imperative to understand how hardware specialization paradigms can benefit HPC. An essential question revolves around estimating the theoretical performance of an optimally specialized architecture without requiring extensive hardware development expertise and efforts.
